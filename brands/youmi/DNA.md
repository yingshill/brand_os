# Brand DNA: 天下有咪

> A bilingual (CN/EN) curator brand for cat owners — product picks, care knowledge, trends, and lifestyle.
> Position: the trustworthy almanac for everything cat. Not an influencer cat; a curator's voice.

---

## Voice & Tone
- **Curator, not salesperson.** Speak as a knowledgeable friend who has tested the things. Recommendations are reasoned, not breathless.
- **Warm + authoritative.** The 天下有咪 name carries a classical, encyclopedic feel — voice should match: confident, calm, evidence-aware. Never cutesy baby-talk, never hype.
- **Bilingual native.** CN copy for XHS reads as written-natively in Chinese (not translated). EN copy for Instagram reads as written-natively in English. Idioms, sentence rhythm, and reference points adapt to each language — same insight, two voices.
- **Show your reasoning.** When recommending a product, say *why* — the cat's behavior it fits, the failure mode it avoids, the tradeoff vs. cheaper options.
- **Admit limits.** "Worked for my cat, may not for yours." "Vet-backed sources cited." Trust > volume.

## Target Audience
- **Cat owners — new (0–2 years).** Anxious, over-researching, want a trusted shortlist. Highest conversion intent.
- **Cat owners — experienced.** Already have opinions; come for niche/advanced picks (multi-cat setups, senior cat care, behavior issues).
- **Cat-curious / prospective owners.** Browsing before committing. Long nurture cycle but high LTV.
- **Geo:** CN urban (XHS) + EN-speaking diaspora & global (Instagram). Skew 25–40, predominantly female, urban apartment dwellers.

## Content Pillars
1. **Product picks** (food, toys, litter, gear, furniture) — affiliate-ready. Always include reasoning + alternative at different price points.
2. **Care knowledge** (health, behavior, vet-backed tips) — evergreen, SEO-friendly. Cite sources where possible.
3. **Trending topics / cat news / memes** — reactive, riding cultural moments. Keep the curator voice even when the topic is silly.
4. **Owner lifestyle** (apartment setup, travel with cat, multi-cat households, adoption stories) — aspirational + practical.

## Default Channels (overrides global LinkedIn-centric defaults)
- **Xiaohongshu post** — primary CN channel. Cover image + body copy. Tone: warm, narrative, image-led.
- **Instagram carousel** — primary EN channel for product picks and care knowledge. 5–8 slides.
- **Instagram Reel script** — short-form video for trends + lifestyle. 15–45 sec.
- **Notion Website entry** — evergreen archive. Long-form "almanac" page that the social posts link back to.

Optional/approval-gated: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, email newsletter.

## Core Keywords (recurring themes)
- Cat products, cat food review, cat toys, litter box, cat tree, cat behavior, cat health, vet advice, multi-cat, indoor cat, kitten care, senior cat, cat-proofing, pet lifestyle, 猫粮测评, 猫用品, 养猫知识, 猫咪行为, 新手养猫.

## Anti-patterns (do NOT do)
- Don't anthropomorphize the cat as the speaker (this is a curator brand, not a cat-character brand).
- Don't use sponsored-looking language without disclosure.
- Don't recommend products you wouldn't use on your own cat.
- Don't pad with filler ("As a cat owner, I know…") — get to the substance.
- Don't translate word-for-word between CN and EN; rewrite for each language.
