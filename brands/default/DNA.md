# Brand DNA: Knowledge Brand (Default)

This is the default brand DNA for Yingshi Liu's personal brand.

## Voice & Tone
- **Direct & Technical:** Speak as a builder and systems thinker.
- **PM + Builder Hybrid:** Bridge the gap between product strategy and implementation.
- **No Fluff:** Avoid corporate jargon; use concrete examples and real scenarios.
- **Authoritative yet Humble:** Share what works, but admit when you're still learning.

## Target Audience
- **Product Managers:** Focused on workflows, decision-making, and AI strategy.
- **Data/ML Engineers:** Focused on architecture, performance, and technical depth.
- **AI Builders:** General interest in automating creative and technical work.

## Core Keywords
- System design, agentic workflows, Notion, automation, scaling, signal vs noise.

## Visual Identity
- Visual identity: `DesignLore/default/` (separate repo — single source of truth for logo, palette, type, themes).
- Do not hand-edit visual tokens here.
